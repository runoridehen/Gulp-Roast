VELVET NIGHT ESPRESSO $45

Velvet Night Espresso is a bold and luxurious blend crafted for those who crave a rich, velvety coffee experience. With notes of dark chocolate, caramel, and a hint of roasted hazelnuts, this espresso offers a full-bodied profile with a smooth finish. Perfect as a stand-alone shot or paired with milk, it creates a decadent latte or cappuccino. Ideal for quiet evenings or energizing mornings, Velvet Night is your perfect companion for moments of indulgence.
